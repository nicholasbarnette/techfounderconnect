Tech Founder Connect

Where passion, talent, and expertise meet.

Our Mission

Tech Founder Connect is a platform started with the goal of helping people build their ideas. We make the process of finding technical talent for business owners simple and straightforward. We host events combining informational, social, and networking aspects for business owners and technologists alike. Our vision is to make a more connected world, one where technologists are not siloed by their companies, business owners are not limited by their personal connections, and people's ideas come to life.

Our Program

Initmate

We recruit a small number of individuals to take part in our program to ensure an intimate setting that is optimized for learning and networking.

Accessible

Unlike many other conferences, ours is free to attend. The catch is that we ask that afterward you contribute what you feel the conference is worth.

Rewarding

Your contributions will partially be used for philanthropy and we will ensure that we are enacting positive change in our communities.

Story

I worked as a software developer at Amazon for a while before quitting and starting my own startup. During my time in the startup world I found many founders with great ideas and no access to technical resources. I also serve as an admin of a large community of passionate programmers looking for projects. I decided why not put these people together and get founders access to technical resources and technical folks access to projects and startups.

Our Program

1) We interview both founders and technical talent alike. We look for determined, resilient, and visionary founders who can execute and techies with the ability to build out full applications.

2) We match founders with a team of one to two techies who will build out the application under our guidance. We will coach our technical talent along for 12 months.

3) After the initial 12 month app period, we provide support in the form of consultations and mentorship for another 6 month period.
